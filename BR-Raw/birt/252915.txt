Bug 252915 Chart background image is repeated
Build ID: M20080911-1700 Steps To Reproduce: 1.Create a report and insert a chart.
2.Go to Advanced properties and add a "Background image" to the chart-background-background image.
Hint: it is easy to see the problem if the background image is less than 1/4 of the chart size.
3.Set "Background repeat" to "No Repeat".
The chart still shows the image repeated.
Image repeats across and down the chart.
