Description: Refresh library will change cube dimension name.
Build number:2.2.0.v20070614 Steps to reproduce: 1.New a library with data source, dataset and cube.
2.New a report.
3.Drag cube from library to report and insert a xtab based on the cube.

5.Back to report and in Outline, select report, right click and select refresh library.
