Controls in shell blocked by a modal shell still scroll via the scroll wheel
I20080415-1646
The following snippet shows the problem.
When the shells are up, click on the shell underneith.
Note that it doesn't get focus.
Now (while the mouse is still over this bottom shell) use the scroll the scrollwheel.
