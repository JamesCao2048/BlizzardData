GTK-BIDI: RTL support in ScrollBar.
Created  attachment 80676   [details]
Screenshots
Build ID: 3.4M2
Steps To Reproduce:
1.Run aplication with RTL-oriented Scrollable (Table, List, etc.) and resize it so, that horizontal scroll bar appears.
Leftmost position of ScrollBar corresponds to rightmost position of scrollable, and vice versa.
Moving bar causes moving viewport to the opposite direction.
More information:
Range of horizontal scroll bar with RTL orientation is set to inverted mode.
This setting isn't needed for lates GTK versions.
Attachments contain screenshots and patch with suggested changes.
