Spinner widget can't enter the number from IME of the japanese-language input setting.
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; ja; rv:1.9.1.5) Gecko/20091102 Firefox/3.5.5 (.NET CLR 3.5.30729)
Build Identifier: I20091030-1201
The Spinner widget can't enter the number from IME of the japanese-language input setting.
Spinner widget refuses the normal-width figure input from IME.
Reproducible: Always
Steps to Reproduce:
1.Open the preferences.
2.It selects [General] > [Startup and Shutdown] > [Workspaces] from a left menu.
3.[Number of recent workspaces to remember] is selected, IME is started, and the normal-width figure is input.
