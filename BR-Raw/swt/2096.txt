AddFontResourceEx missing for ANSI in OS
According to MSDN, AddFontResourceEx is implemented as both Unicode and ANSI.
However, it is unsupported in Windows 95/98/Me, only included in Windows 2000 and later.
The older AddFontResource is implemented as both Unicode and ANSI.
We currently implement AddFontResourceExW.
Also, RemoveFontResourceExA is not implemented.
