Editor/View switcher: 1 key press forwards by 3 items
I20090217-2200
On Cocoa, when you press and hold Command and the press F6, the editor switcher popup table appears with the second item selected (good).
When you press F6 a second time, the selection jumps to the fifth row (expected: jump to third).
When you release Command, something similar to  bug 265393  happens: The editor switcher popup opens again (and stays open)
